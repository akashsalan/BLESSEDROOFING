About Us

Built on Trust, Driven by Quality

Blessed Roofing & Construction LLC is a locally owned and operated roofing company serving Amarillo, Texas and the surrounding Panhandle region. Founded by Hugo Garcia, our company was built on a simple promise — deliver honest, high-quality roofing work at prices that respect our customers' budgets.

Hugo brings a deep passion for craftsmanship and a commitment to treating every home like his own. From the first phone call to the final inspection, you'll experience a level of communication, professionalism, and care that sets us apart from the rest.

Comprehensive Roofing & Construction Services

Roof Installation
